1click Games is a free, browser-based mini games platform offering 9+ casual games including Chess, Ludo, Sudoku, Snake, Baagchal, Snake & Ladder, Tic-Tac-Toe, Carrom, and Truth or Dare. All games are 100% client-side — no dedicated server is required for gameplay.

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide
We **do not require** account creation or personal data to play our games. We do not collect:
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Payment or financial information
- Government IDs or sensitive personal data

### 3.2 Automatically Collected Information
When you use our app or website, we may automatically collect:

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Device type & OS version | App compatibility & optimization |
| App version | Bug tracking & crash diagnostics |
| General location (country/region only) | Analytics & content optimization |
| Session duration & game interactions | Improving game design |
| Crash reports & error logs | Debugging & stability |

### 3.3 Local Storage (Your Device Only)
We use your device's **local storage** (localStorage / SharedPreferences) to save:
- Game settings (theme, sound on/off)
- High scores and personal best records
- In-progress game state

This data is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers.

### GDPR (European Users)
If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to:
- Access, correct, or erase your personal data
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

- Know what personal information is collected

- Opt out of the sale of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
