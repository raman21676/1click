🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 1click Games collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best gaming experience:

  • Game Progress: High scores and game state (stored locally in your browser)
  • Preferences: Theme preference (dark/light mode) and sound settings
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about which games are played (via Google Analytics)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, and IP address (for security and optimization)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Saving your game progress and high scores
  • Remembering your preferences (theme, sound)
  • Improving our games and user experience
  • Analyzing website traffic and performance
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser technologies to store data:

LocalStorage

We store game data (scores, preferences) in your browser's LocalStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics (Google Analytics) - to understand how visitors use our site
  • Advertising (Google AdSense) - to serve personalized ads

🍪 How to Control Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, this may affect your experience on our website. Learn more at aboutcookies.org.

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Here's what you should know:

  • Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the Internet
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads

For more information about how Google uses data, visit: How Google uses information from sites that use their services

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Tracks page views, session duration, and user interactions
  • All data is anonymized and aggregated
  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

6. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • We do not store personal information on our servers
  • Game data is stored locally in your browser only
  • We regularly review our security practices

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is suitable for all ages. However:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us
  • Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites:

  • We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites
  • We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit
  • External links are clearly marked and open in new tabs

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your LocalStorage data (through browser settings)
  • Disable cookies in your browser
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time:

  • Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date
  • Significant changes will be highlighted
  • Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance
