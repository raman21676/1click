Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

Quick Summary: We respect your privacy. 1click Games stores game progress locally on your device. We don't collect personal data. When we add ads in the future, ad providers may use cookies as described below.

1. Information We Collect

Local Storage Only

We store the following information locally on your device using your browser's LocalStorage:

  • Game high scores
  • Game settings (sound on/off, theme preferences)
  • Sudoku progress (if you save a game)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics (Coming Soon)

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This collects:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Country (approximate location)

This data is anonymous and aggregated. You can opt-out by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Current Use

Currently, we do not use any cookies except for essential functionality like sound preferences.

Future Use (Advertising)

We plan to display ads using Google AdSense. When implemented, Google may use cookies to:

  • Serve personalized advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud

You can control personalized ads through Google Ads Settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Collected
Google Fonts Typography IP address
GitHub Pages Hosting Access logs (IP, browser)
Google Analytics (planned) Usage statistics Anonymous usage data
Google AdSense (planned) Display advertisements Cookies, device info

4. Children's Privacy

Our games are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

5. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transfer
  • No server-side storage of personal data
  • Local data stays on your device

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear local data: Clear your browser's LocalStorage to remove all game data
  • Opt-out of analytics: Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser
  • Control ads: Manage ad personalization at Google Ads Settings
  • Contact us: Ask questions about this privacy policy

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Displaying a notice on our website
