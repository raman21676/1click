🎯 Carrom Board

Pocket coins, cover the queen, win the game!

📜 How to Play

  • Pocket all your color coins (White/Black)
  • Queen (Red) must be covered - pocket another coin right after
  • Black/White coin = 1 point
  • Queen = 5 points (only if covered)
  • First to 29 points or pocket all coins wins
  • Drag striker to aim, pull back for power
