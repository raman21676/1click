🐅 Baagchal (बाघचाल)

🐅 1
🐐 4
Goat's Turn
Phase: Place Goats (0/4 placed)

📜 Rules

  • 1 Tiger vs 4 Goats
  • Phase 1: Goats place their pieces one by one
  • Phase 2: Players alternate turns moving to adjacent spots
  • Tiger captures by jumping over a goat (like checkers)
  • Goats win if tiger can't move
  • Tiger wins if it captures all goats

🎉 Game Over!

Goats win!
